Self-Help Tools & Activities

Explore our collection of practical tools designed specifically for students to manage stress, anxiety, and promote overall mental wellbeing. Regular practice of these techniques can help you maintain balance during your academic journey.

5-Minute Breathing Exercise

This quick breathing technique can help reduce anxiety before exams or presentations. Practice anywhere, anytime you need to center yourself.

How to practice:
  1. Find a comfortable seated position
  2. Breathe in through your nose for 4 counts
  3. Hold your breath for 2 counts
  4. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 counts
  5. Repeat for 5 minutes

Daily Gratitude Journal

Take a few minutes each day to reflect on positive aspects of your life and academic journey.

Today's Prompts:
  1. What is one thing you learned today that you're grateful for?
  2. Who is someone at school or in your studies who helped you recently?
  3. What is one thing about your education that you appreciate?
