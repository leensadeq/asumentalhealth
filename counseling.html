Find Professional Help

Connecting with the right mental health professional can make a significant difference in your wellbeing. This directory helps you find therapists, counselors, and support groups that specialize in student mental health concerns.

Many professionals offer flexible scheduling and virtual appointments to accommodate busy student schedules. Some services also offer reduced rates for students.

How to Choose the Right Therapist

Consider These Factors:
  • ✅ Specialization: Look for experience with student issues
  • ✅ Approach: Research different therapy methods
  • ✅ Availability: Consider your schedule and time zone
  • ✅ Cost: Check insurance coverage and student discounts
Questions to Ask:
  • ❓ What is your experience with college students?
  • ❓ What therapeutic approaches do you use?
  • ❓ How do you handle crisis situations?
  • ❓ What are your policies on cancellations and rescheduling?
